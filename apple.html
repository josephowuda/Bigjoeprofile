WHAT IS AN APPLE

An apple is a popular and nutritious fruit that grows on the tree known as Malus domestica. It is widely eaten around the world and comes in many varieties such as red, green, and yellow apples.

Description

Apples are usually round in shape with smooth skin. Their color can range from bright red to green or yellow. Inside, they have a crisp, juicy flesh and contain small brown seeds at the center.

Nutritional Value

Apples are rich in important nutrients, including: Vitamins (especially Vitamin C) Fiber (helps digestion) Antioxidants (help protect the body from diseases)

Health Benefits

Eating apples regularly can:

    Improve digestion
    Support heart health
    Help control blood sugar
    Boost the immune system

Uses

Apples can be eaten in many ways:

    Fresh as a snack
    Juiced into apple juice
    Cooked in foods like pies and sauces
    Dried for longer storage
